The Daily Color

Each day of the year is represented by a different color inspired by astrology, numerology, the four zodiac elements, color theory, and the metaphysical powers of color. Studying and contemplating the color of your birthday and the other special dates may provide you with clarity, insight, inspiration, and new perspectives.

The TikTok page @the.daily.color catalogs each daily color with a video slideshow of gorgeous photos, artwork, music, characteristics, and compatible birthdays.

You can now purchase a Daily Color Calendar from @JCJArtDesign on Etsy in either a printed format, or a digital only copy which also includes digital desktop wallpapers for each month!
