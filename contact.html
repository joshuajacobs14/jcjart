About Joshua

Joshua Jacobs is a queer artist, designer, and polyglot.

Josh studies Art and Design: Drawing and Painting Emphasis at Utah Valley University. Josh has been passionately making art since childhood.

Josh has landed paintings in several local and state art galleries as well as commissioned graphic design work for organizations such as Harmons, Brigham Young University's Nursing Program, Utah Valley University, and Sunroc.

Josh also spends time as an LGBT+ activist. Josh is on the Board of Trustees of Provo Pride and is also the Outreach Chair for Spectrum: Queer Student Alliance at UVU. Josh enjoys creating art with queer themes.
