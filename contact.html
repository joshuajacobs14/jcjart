About JCJ

Name: JC Jacobs (Also known as JCJ, Josh, Jorts, queerjorts, Juice, Jonch, and Jem)

Pronouns: They/them, she/her, or he/him

JCJ is a queer artist/designer/content creator from Utah. They graduated from Utah Valley University in Art & Design. They started selling artwork in 2017 under the business name "Art Joshua Studio". They have since rebranded to "JCJ Art & Design".
