About JCJ

Hello! My name is J.C. Jacobs and I'm a queer nonbinary artist and freelance graphic designer. I'm a Virgo, I’m lactose intolerant, and I use any pronouns but prefer "they/them".

I have a degree in Art & Design from Utah Valley University but I also briefly studied business, video game design, web development, web design, linguistics, American Sign Language, and Spanish. At one point I was trying to learn 12 languages, but right now I'm only fluent in English and Tagalog.

I was raised Mormon with my ten siblings, but at age 23 I came out of the closet and left the LDS Church.

In 2015 I started selling artwork as “Art Joshua Studio” but I renamed it “JCJ Art Design” in 2022. I’ve sold LGBT+ themed artwork and merchandise at pride festivals, markets, and online.

My artwork is influenced by my upbringing and queer identity. Beyond telling my own story, I also try to create art that uplifts the LGBT community and defends human rights.
