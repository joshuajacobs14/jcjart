About JCJ

Jaspen Jacobs is a queer nonbinary artist, graphic designer, writer, and comedian from Utah. They are lactose-intolerant and a Virgo.

Jaspen studied Art & Design at Utah Valley University. They have displayed paintings and sculptures at art galleries throughout Utah. They have sold artwork prints, cards, and stickers at farmers markets, pride festivals, and online since 2017. Their artwork is influenced by their upbringing and queer identity. Beyond telling their own story, they also strive to create art that uplifts the LGBTQ+ community and defends human rights.

Jaspen started doing stand-up comedy in 2024. They are always looking for new places to sell artwork, or perform stand-up. Please email jaspenj@hotmail.com for inquiries.
