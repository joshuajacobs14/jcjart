About JCJ

JCJ is a queer nonbinary artist from Utah. They also go by the names JC, Jaspen, Jorts, queerjorts, and uncutjorts. They graduated from Utah Valley University in Art & Design. They started selling artwork in 2017 under the business name "Art Joshua Studio''. They have since rebranded to "JCJ Art & Design".

JCJ designs and creates stickers, paintings, prints, greeting cards, bookmarks, magnets, merch, earrings, and more. They started selling LGBTQ artwork at pride festivals and farmers markets in Utah. When the COVID pandemic started, they had to stop doing in-person festivals. They moved online to Etsy and Redbubble. Ever since, it has been a struggle to make money. But every sale is meaningful. Every “Trans Rights” sticker on a water bottle means more representation and visibility for the LGBTQ+ community.
