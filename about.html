About JCJ

J.C. Jacobs is a queer nonbinary artist, designer, and content creator who uses the pronouns they/them/their, ze/zem/zeir, she/her/hers, or he/him/his.

They studied Art & Design at Utah Valley University.

J.C. was raised LDS along with their ten siblings. At age 23 they came out of the closet as gay and left the LDS church. Four years later they came out as nonbinary.

In 2015 they started selling artwork as "Art Joshua Studio", later renamed "JCJ Art & Design" in 2022. They have sold LGBTQ+ themed artwork and merchandise at pride festivals, markets, conventions, and online.

Their artwork is influenced by their upbringing and queer identity. Their work has a goal of uplifting the LGBT community, and defending human rights.
