About Joshua

Hello! My name is Joshua Jacobs, and I am a freelance artist located in Provo, Utah. I've always had a passion for art. Even as a kid I loved to draw, paint and color. I primarily love to paint, but I also like to experiment with other mediums. I enjoy ceramics, web design, graphic design and video game design. I love art more than anything. It's what I want to do with my life.

I've had artwork featured in multiple galleries, & competitions. I've placed 3rd place for a painting at the Bountiful Davis Art Gallery. I've won best of division (among other awards) at a county art show. I've had my work featured in an Ad for Harmons Grocery Store (and was recognized in the Newspaper abou it). I designed a T-shirt for Brigham Young University's Nursing Program. I've also designed several video games. Two years in a row I worked on games that acheived first place at Weber State University's Summer Video Game Camp.

I am currently studying Art & Design at Utah Valley University. My emphasis is in Drawing, Painting & Print Making. I have a minor in Digital Media. I work part time as a Sandwich Artist.

Being a gay man in Utah and growing up in the LDS Church, I've been through a lot and I've had a variety of experiences. I want my voice to be heard, whether it's through my paintings, writings, videos or other platforms. I want to help make the world a better place for everyone. To me, art is what brings us together and shows us that we're not all that different. I think we all have more in common than we sometimes seem to think.

Be sure to follow me on Social Media to see my latest posts and updates!!
